Math Challenge

If the sum of three consecutive odd numbers is 63, what is the smallest number?

2/6

Let's Solve This!

Let's call the first odd number x

The next two consecutive odd numbers would be:

x + 2 and x + 4
3/6

Set Up the Equation

The sum of all three equals 63:

x + (x + 2) + (x + 4) = 63
4/6

Simplify

Combine like terms:

3x + 6 = 63
5/6

Solve for x

Subtract 6 from both sides:

3x = 57

Divide by 3:

x = 19
6/6

The Answer!

The smallest number is:

19

The three consecutive odd numbers are:
19, 21, and 23
